# Task 2 approval notes

## Project idea

### End-user
Any person who wants to keep a personal catalog of books and movies they have already read or watched.

### Problem
People often forget what they have already watched or read, what they liked or disliked, and what they would recommend to others. Keeping this information in memory or random notes is inconvenient.

### Product idea in one short sentence
A personal web app for storing books and movies with ratings, comments, sorting, and filtering.

### Core feature
Add a book or movie with title, rating, date, and comment, then browse the collection with sorting and filtering.

## Implementation plan

### Version 1
A functioning personal collection app that lets users:
- add a book or movie,
- store title, rating, date, and comment,
- browse the collection,
- sort by rating, date, and title.

### Version 2
Build on top of Version 1 by adding:
- filtering by type,
- search by title/comment,
- favorites,
- edit/delete,
- statistics,
- better UI,
- dockerized deployment.
